About Royal Events Caterers

Who We Are

Royal Events Caterers Limited is a team of dedicated, artistic and culturally inspired chefs who provide exceptional food and beverages services. We help numerous successful companies inspire employees every week and grow healthy workplaces.

We have a multifaceted reputation for providing daily meals, running company canteens, preparing offsite meals from our fully equipped kitchen and transforming company events through our exceptional, delicious and affordable meals.

How We Do It

We value long term client partnerships built on trust. We understand that treating employees well is the foundation of great companies.

Our daily nutritious breakfast and lunch programs ensure this becomes a reality. We also enrich the dining experience by providing professional waiters and waitresses to make every meal service exciting and memorable.

Our Values

  • Timely service delivery
  • Consistent quality & adequate meal portions
  • Flexible and versatile menus
  • High quality & tasty meals
  • Unlimited customer focus

Our Vision

We believe that access to affordable, high quality and nutrient-rich meals should be part of everyday life. Our mission is to make this a reality for individuals and corporates.
